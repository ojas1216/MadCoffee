Our Story

More than
just coffee.

Born in 2021 from a simple obsession — making every cup the best part of someone's day. Mad Coffee opened its doors in Tallaght with one promise: premium specialty coffee, served with genuine warmth and that signature smile.

We source single-origin beans from family farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala, roasted to perfection and pulled with precision by our passionate team of baristas who treat every shot as a craft.

This is your neighbourhood café done properly. Come in, slow down, and start your day with a smile.

The Experience

Every morning
begins with
intention.

We believe that how you start your day shapes everything that follows. That's why every cup we craft is made with care, precision, and a genuine desire to make you smile.

The Origin

Beans from
the world's
finest farms.

We source directly from small-holder farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala — paying above fair-trade rates, building relationships, and bringing you coffee with a story worth telling.

The Craft

Crafted by
hands that
truly care.

Our baristas aren't just trained — they're passionate. Each extraction is dialled in fresh, each pour is intentional. The result is consistency that tastes like dedication in every sip.
